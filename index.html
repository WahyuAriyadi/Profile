Profile Overview

Backend roots, cloud focus, AI curiosity

The short version of how I got here — and what I'm building toward. Skills, projects, certifications and contact details each live on their own page, linked below.

I'm a fresh graduate in Informatics Engineering from Universitas Sebelas Maret (UNS), finishing with a 3.65/4.0 GPA. My path combined backend development, cloud infrastructure, and applied NLP rather than picking just one lane.

I trained through Bangkit Academy's Cloud Computing Path (graduated with distinction) and AWS re/Start, and hold both AWS and Google Cloud certifications. As part of Bangkit, I led a cross-functional team building VoyageEducation, a career-recommendation app deployed on GCP Cloud Run.

My thesis applied IndoBERT and BiLSTM to aspect-based sentiment analysis on Indonesian bank reviews — a low-resource NLP problem close to home. These days I'm building infrastructure that's genuinely useful for small Indonesian businesses, including a Terraform module inspired by my own family's food stall.
